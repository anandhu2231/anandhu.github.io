About me

I'm Anandhu S., a Cybersecurity Engineer and Pentester passionate about protecting digital ecosystems while creating visually compelling user experiences. With a strong foundation in cybersecurity, I've honed my expertise in threat detection, vulnerability assessment, and pentesting, safeguarding systems and applications from evolving cyber threats. My hands-on experience spans vulnerability management and in-depth testing to identify and mitigate critical security risks.

In addition to my technical skills in security, I have a creative flair as a Web and Graphic Designer. I bring concepts to life through user-focused design, blending aesthetics and functionality. My proficiency with tools like Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD, and Figma allows me to craft engaging, visually polished websites and graphics that enhance user engagement.

With a unique blend of cybersecurity insight and design creativity, I’m committed to both securing and beautifying the digital landscape. Whether protecting systems from threats or creating visually engaging content, I bring a dedicated, innovative approach to every project I take on.
